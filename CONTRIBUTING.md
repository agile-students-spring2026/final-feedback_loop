# Team Norms

### Communication 
- We agree to remain active and available through Discord team chat by responding to messages within 12-24 hours
- If a team member will be absent for a standup meeting, they must notify the team in advance and review meeting notes
- Meeting minutes will be documented for team members to refer to as neccesary   

### Participation 
- We agree to evenly distribute the responsibilites regarding code, documentation, and overall project management 
- All tasks will be completed by the agreed upon team deadline
    - If a team member is running behind, they will notify the team with fair notice and ask for help
- We agree to use GitHub Issues to track responsibilities and progress

### Collaboration Standards
- We agree to always test our code before merging with the main branch and handle merge conflicts as they appear
- We agree to collaborate and problem solve effectively for the success of our project 


## Conflict Resolution 

In the event that a team member is not attending several meetings, continuosly missing deadlines, or not participating in team communication/collaboration: 

1. Addressing the issue: We will respectfully address the issue with the individual and see if they need extra support.

If the problem persists or the individual shows no effort to rectifying the issue: 

2. Document treatment plan: We will document specific, measurable expectations on how we plan to remedy this issue and what the individual plans to do make up for the shortcoming. 

If we believe we are unable to address the issue on our own: 

3. Escalate the issue: Reach out to Professor Bloomberg and course admin with our continue concern and the steps we've already taken to address the issue.
